1. Simulating COVID-19 with Brownian Dynamics

A simple contagion model was obtained, when making certain analogies between a human population and a two-dimensional coloidal suspension system under brownian motion. Hermosillo's population density (a city located in Sonora, Mexico) is taken into consideration. In this model sanitary measures, such as Stay Home and Keep your Distance, are taken into consideration.
